ABOUT US

This is a people business.





When we first sit down with a client whether for business or personal business, we ask ourselves: do we share a common vision of where they want to go? And secondly, would we enjoy going for a drink together? Knowing how involved client-firm relationships can be, chemistry and ‘fit’ are key to a successful journey.

People and their projects turn us on. Have something interesting or bold you want to do? Bring it on, because we love being a part of it, and we are here to support you all the way – with cross-disciplinary expertise, creative ideas and rock-solid advice.

MMT has been cheering on and supporting people and their projects for close to 35 years. Many of our clients started with us as start-ups and have gone on to become multi-million dollar enterprises.

Fit is also about giving you easy access to our senior advisors. Fit means taking the time to listen and truly understand your needs. Fit is about delivering not off the shelf products, but the right set of solutions custom-tailored to the needs of you and or your business.

People and their passions are what make this business fun for us, and what’s really nice about a firm of our size is the opportunity to share not only strategies and ideas, but also laughs and good times.

Have an idea or a question about a project or task you’d like our input on? Feel free to write us or give us a call, because we love talking with people.
